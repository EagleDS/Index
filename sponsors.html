Real Impact, Real Communities

We Work Where
It Matters Most.

Eagle DS works actively with Oranga Tamariki, Barnardos and local schools, delivering combat sport as a tool for youth development, anger management, confidence and whānau reconnection.

A partnership here isn't just brand exposure — it's a stake in genuine community outcomes across South Auckland and a national network of clubs.

"One of our most meaningful cases involved a 13-year-old who had become a gang leader. Through Eagle DS he was able to manage his anger, rebuild his family relationships, and refocus on being a father. This is what combat sport, done right, can do."

Eagle DS operates two distinct partnership opportunities. Each delivers different value to different partners — but both are part of the same mission. Read both, and choose where you fit best. Or support both.

Arm One
Combat Games Series
National Events Circuit
New Zealand's leading light-contact competition circuit — bringing together 50+ clubs across multiple disciplines for structured, development-first events with national reach and livestream coverage.
ReachesMartial artists nationwide, sports parents & coaches, combat-sports fans via livestream, 50+ affiliated clubs and their communities.
Best fitSports & active-lifestyle brands, energy drinks, nutrition & supplements, sporting goods & apparel, brands wanting national event exposure.
Arm Two
Combat & Wellness Facility
Auckland Training & Health Hub
A permanent Auckland training facility and wellness centre offering martial arts coaching, clinical nutrition, mindfulness and holistic health programmes for individuals, families and youth.
ReachesAuckland families and active individuals, youth and at-risk young people, health-conscious professionals, corporate wellness clients.
Best fitHealth, wellness & nutrition brands, health insurance & medical providers, corporates with a community/CSR focus, local Auckland businesses.
