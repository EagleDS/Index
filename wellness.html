Why It Matters

Rare Rigour
in Combat Sport.

Most combat sports organisations treat health as an afterthought. Eagle DS was built the other way around. Carrie Sanders — a Registered Nurse with frontline clinical experience and a qualified Clinical Nutritionist — designs and oversees the wellness architecture behind everything we do.

For at-risk and vulnerable young people especially, having a nurse in the room — even informally — changes the nature of the environment entirely. It is safer, calmer, and held to a clinical standard.

0
concussions on record.

Across five national tournaments and more than 450 matches. Not luck — the product of clinical oversight, controlled-contact standards, and a culture that develops people rather than damaging them.
