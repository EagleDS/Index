About Our Supporter

Seven Days.
One Reset.

Sanctum 7 runs seven-day luxury wellness retreats in exclusive locales, built for anyone navigating the pressures of work and family life who needs a genuinely restorative break.

Their flagship retreat takes place at Earnscleugh Castle — as featured on Grand Designs — limited to just 12 guests across 10 retreats a year, commencing late spring 2026.

"We'd love to welcome you. Your body, mind and heart will thank you."

Sanctum 7

What They Offer

Small Groups.
Fully Personalised.

Every retreat is built around four pillars — care that's exclusive, restorative, personalised, and nourishing.
