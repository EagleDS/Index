National Infrastructure

Three Pillars.
One Standard.

Eagle DS isn't a single venue — it's the infrastructure layer for combat sport in New Zealand: where athletes are developed, where they compete, and who governs the contest.

01
Academy Architecture

A structured, full-pathway development system — from a child's first pad class to national and international competition. The talent pipeline starts here.

02
Tournament Software

An independent event platform: registration, live scoring, real-time brackets, and mat management — engineered specifically for combat sport, not borrowed from elsewhere.

03
Officials Education

Formal referee and judge accreditation that drives consistency across every event. A credentialed standard the whole ecosystem can trust.
