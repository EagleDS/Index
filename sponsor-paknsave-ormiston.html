About Our Supporter

Our Neighbours
in East Tāmaki.

PAK'nSAVE Ormiston sits right in the heart of Ormiston Town Centre — part of the same community Eagle DS serves every day. Their aim is simple: New Zealand's lowest food prices, delivered by always looking for ways to keep costs down and pass the savings on to local whānau.

They contribute to PAK'nSAVE's broader Here for NZ programme — a commitment to being part of the communities they trade in, not just a presence in them.

"What's fair for our customers is what's good for New Zealand — being here for New Zealand is in our DNA."

PAK'nSAVE Ormiston
