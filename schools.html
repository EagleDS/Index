The Coach

Who Is
Dafydd Sanders

Your students will be in the room with one of New Zealand's most credentialed combat sports coaches — a world-ranked competitor who has spent 24 years turning that experience into a system that develops real people.

Dafydd Sanders
Founder · Eagle DS · International Competitor & Coach

Dafydd Sanders is not a motivational speaker with a sporting background. He is an active, practising combat sports system builder — someone who has spent three decades at the highest levels of competitive martial arts and 24 years engineering the methodology that transfers elite performance capability to young people.

He competed for New Zealand across multiple Olympic campaign cycles, reaching World #10 in Taekwondo. His competitive record spans WAKO Worlds kickboxing and K1, IMMAF Worlds MMA, and a Karate All Japan Championship title in 2010 — alongside being named Māori Sports Person of the Year at the University of Auckland in 2005. He understands, from the inside, what it takes to perform at the absolute limit — and what happens when you don't have the mental architecture to handle it.

What he brings to your school is not a story about sport. It is a proven system for building students who are mentally durable, focused under pressure, and capable of sustained high performance — in the classroom, in competition, and in life.

02
The Programme

Two Sessions.
Lasting Impact.

Each session is standalone and delivers immediate value — or they can be run back to back for a full school day experience. Both are designed and delivered personally by Dafydd Sanders.

Session One · Classroom
The High-Performance Mindset Talk

An honest, direct presentation on what separates students who perform under pressure from those who don't — drawn from 31 years at the elite level of combat sports and 24 years coaching the next generation. This is not a motivational talk. It is a practical framework your students can use immediately.

What elite athletes do differently — and why it applies to every student in the room
How to perform under pressure rather than collapse under it
The habits that separate high achievers from everyone else — and how to build them
Dafydd's personal journey through adversity at world level — and what it taught him about mental durability
Age-appropriate, direct, and built for a high school audience
Session Two · Field / Hall
The Combat Sports Workshop

A hands-on introductory session in Olympic Taekwondo and kickboxing fundamentals. No experience required. Designed to put students in a controlled physical challenge — where the lessons of the talk become immediately tangible. Students feel what focus, discipline, and technique actually mean in practice.

Foundational movement, stance, and striking technique
Partner drills that build communication and trust under pressure
Safe, structured, and inclusive — every fitness level welcome
Students leave with a physical experience of what high performance feels like
A pathway offered to any student who wants to go further

Session length is flexible — both sessions can be tailored to your timetable, from a 45-minute period through to a half or full-day format. Dafydd works around your schedule, not the other way around.

03
The Outcomes

What Your
Students Take Away

Every school brings in external speakers. What separates this from a generic workshop is the depth of the system behind it — and the credibility of the person delivering it.

1
A Practical Framework for Performing Under Pressure
Not inspiration — tools. Students leave with a concrete understanding of how high performers manage pressure, build focus, and recover from failure. These are skills they can apply in exams, in sport, and in every high-stakes situation they face.
2
Physical Experience of Discipline and Focus
The workshop session doesn't just talk about discipline — it puts students inside it. They experience what it feels like to focus completely on a physical task, to listen precisely, and to improve within a single session. That experience is visceral and memorable.
3
Exposure to Elite-Level Thinking
Many students have never been in the room with someone who has competed at world level in anything. Dafydd's presence — and the way he thinks and speaks about performance — raises the ceiling of what students believe is possible for themselves.
4
A Pathway for Students Who Want More
Students who connect with the programme are offered a clear pathway into Eagle DS — from introductory classes through to competitive training and the Young Adult Coach Leadership Academy. This is a genuine developmental opportunity, not a sales pitch.
5
Something Your Prospectus Can Point To
A programme delivered by a world-ranked New Zealand international and 24-year coaching veteran is a genuine differentiator for your school. It signals that you invest in the kind of development that shapes character, not just curriculum.
