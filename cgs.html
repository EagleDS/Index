For Clubs & Coaches

Every Style.
Every Level.

You don't need to be elite to enter. You don't need to have competed before. The CGS is built as a genuine entry point — the place where anyone from any discipline takes their first step onto the competitive mat safely. For those who want to go further, the pathway to elite runs right through here.

Your club keeps

  • Your name, your colours, your coaches, your identity
  • Your athletes — they compete representing you
  • Your training methods and your community
  • Full credit for every fighter you develop

We provide

  • A safe, sanctioned platform — any style, any level, open entry
  • Certified officials trained to one consistent standard
  • Registration, draws, live scoring and event production
  • Livestream coverage and a national stage for your athletes
